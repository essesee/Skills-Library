## Audience Profiles

### Clubs
- Emphasis: Agent workflow improvements, member experience, time savings
- Avoid: Technical details, architecture, internal tooling
- Format: Problem-first, impact-focused talking points

### Leadership
- Emphasis: Strategic progress, ROI indicators, risk mitigation
- Avoid: Implementation details, story-level granularity
- Format: Theme-level with business metrics

### Engineering
- Emphasis: Technical achievements, architecture improvements, debt reduction
- Avoid: Business framing they already know
- Format: What changed, why, and what's next

### All-Hands
- Emphasis: Mix of business impact + technical highlights, team recognition
- Avoid: Deep dives on any one area
- Format: Narrative with 3-4 themes max

## Demo-Worthy Signals
### Always Include
- Features with customer-visible impact
- Significant performance improvements with metrics
- Items leadership specifically asked about

### Usually Skip
- Internal tooling changes with no user impact
- Minor bug fixes unless they were high-visibility
- Refactoring without observable behavior change
